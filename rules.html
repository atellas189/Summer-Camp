Mountside Summer Camp

Rules:

  1. No wandering off
  2. Stick with your buddy
  3. Listen to camp leaders
  4. Stay hydrated
  5. Bring a snack
  6. Bring closed toed shoes
  7. Have fun!

Here at Mountside, our top priority is safety. We have plenty of staff keeping the children safe from any dangerous wildlife. We also have some of the most safe equipment for our activities. We want our campers to have fun while being safe.

All medicine will be turned in to one of the camp leaders and brought anywhere we go in case of an emergency. We also ask that campers turn in their phones and valuables before we start each day to prevent those items from getting lost.
